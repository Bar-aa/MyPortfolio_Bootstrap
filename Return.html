Return Policy

Effective Date: March 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for shopping with Baraa's Portfolio. This Return Policy outlines the conditions under which products purchased from our website can be returned or exchanged.

2. Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must be unused and in the same condition that you received them. The item must also be in the original packaging.

3. Non-returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable. These include gift cards, downloadable software, and items marked as non-returnable on the product page.

4. Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at support@baraaportfolio.com within 30 days of receiving your order. Once approved, we will provide you with a return shipping label and further instructions.

5. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within a few business days.

6. Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item, please follow the same return process. Once the original item is returned, we will ship the replacement item.
