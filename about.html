About me:

Hello! I'm Baraa Shelbaya, a backend developer passionate about building scalable and efficient web applications. I specialize in Python and JavaScript, and I'm always eager to enhance my skills and contribute to meaningful projects. I enjoy creating user-focused applications and ensuring a seamless experience for users.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at An-Najah National University. My academic background has given me a solid foundation in data structures, algorithms, and backend development. During my training and coursework, I gained hands-on experience in developing software and web applications.

My experience as a volunteer with organizations like Tomorrow’s Youth Organization (TYO) and Aḥfād al-Jazarī has given me a broader perspective on life, strengthened my teamwork and leadership abilities, and enhanced my communication skills. Giving back to the community is something I truly value and am passionate about.

As I move forward in my career, my goal is to become a highly skilled backend engineer, continuously improving my expertise in scalable systems, cloud technologies, and API development. I'm always looking for new opportunities to apply my skills and make a meaningful impact through technology.
